About Us

TENCORE is a Ukrainian company created by a team of energetic and highly qualified engineers to develop solutions and manufacture robotic platforms that replace and facilitate human participation in tasks or missions.

Tencore's mission: to design solutions and manufacture robotic platforms that replace human involvement in tasks and save lives.

    Our goal and motto is: "LIFE LOGISTICS", because our solutions:
  • 1. They help to save and preserve life;
  • 2. Provide logistics on the conflict lines;
  • 3. They replace human resources during tasks or missions.

We implement each object in compliance with all the recommendations and goals of the Client.
